How it works

Simple for artists, effortless for venues, instant for fans.

1

Artist performs, fans find them

The artist displays their QR code and announces from the stage: "Support Us Tonight on TrueFans CONNECT Right Now!" Fans nearby can also find the artist automatically via geo-location on TrueFans CONNECT.

2

Fans scan, search, or get located

Fans scan the QR code, or simply visit TrueFans CONNECT and get matched to the artist via geo-location. Pick an amount, pay securely from their phone. No app needed — works in any browser in seconds.

3

Everyone earns

Artists keep their show fee plus 80% of donations. Venues and referrers earn 2.5% commissions across two tiers. Everyone wins.
